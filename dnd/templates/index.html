What is D&D?


In Dungeons & Dragons, players unite to form an adventuring party, delving into fantastical realms as they embark on grand quests and progress through experience levels. Acting as both referee and storyteller, the Dungeon Master, also known as the DM, guides the game. In D&D, traditional concepts of winning and losing take a back seat.

At its core, Dungeons & Dragons is a narrative-driven game where storytelling takes precedence, and dice serve as helpful tools. Every aspect, from your character's appearance to their actions and the unfolding events, is entirely within your control.
